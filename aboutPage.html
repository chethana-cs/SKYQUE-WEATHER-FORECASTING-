ABOUT

Skyesque is a weather forecasting website designed to provide users with accurate, real-time weather information, personalized forecasts, and a variety of weather-related features.
Below is a detailed overview of Skyesque, including its features, technology stack, and user experience considerations.

Overview :

Skyesque aims to deliver comprehensive weather forecasts and related information to a diverse audience, including general users, outdoor enthusiasts, travelers, and professionals who rely on weather data for their activities.
The website is designed to be user-friendly, responsive, and informative, offering a seamless experience across different devices.

2.Personalization and Customization
